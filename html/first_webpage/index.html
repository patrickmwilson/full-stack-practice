Grilled Chicken Tacos

Description

A classic mexican dish enjoyed by everyone. Charcoal grilled chicken, avocado, and salsa. What more could you want?

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • Avocado
  • Corn tortillas
  • Limes
  • White onion
  • Cabbage
  • Salsa
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken seasoning

Directions

  1. Rub the chicken breast with olive oil and chicken seasoning
  2. Place chicken on medium heat grill, flip every 5 minutes until internal temperature of 165Â° F
  3. Chop up cabbage
  4. Peel and slice avocados
  5. Dice onions
  6. Dice cooked chicken
  7. Combine it all in a tortilla! Squeeze a lime onto it for extra flavor
